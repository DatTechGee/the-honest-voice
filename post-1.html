Green Work Day: My Experience Being Interviewed by NTA

Today, I participated in Green Work Day alongside my lecturer, Dr. Godswill Okiyi, as we went around the school to promote environmental cleanliness and sustainability.

During the exercise, I was interviewed by Nigerian Television Authority (NTA). The interview focused on the importance of Green Work Day, environmental responsibility within the school community, and the role students play in maintaining a clean and healthy environment.

This experience was particularly meaningful to me as a Public Relations student, as it provided a real-life opportunity to engage with the media and communicate key messages to a wider audience. It also highlighted how public relations supports environmental awareness, community engagement, and positive institutional image.

The Green Work Day exercise was carried out under the guidance of Dr. Godswill Okiyi, and it strengthened my confidence in public speaking and media interaction while reinforcing the importance of environmental responsibility.
